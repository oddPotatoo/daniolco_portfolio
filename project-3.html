Sixpent Careers and Innovations Page

A Wordpress-based website for the company, Sixpent Inc. It introduces about company and showcases some of their projects. Collaborated with a team to improve the UI/UX design of the website.

Live Link

Project Overview

As part of the team tasked with enhancing the Sixpent Website, I collaborated on the redesign of key pages, including the Careers and Innovations sections. Using WordPress and Elementor, we conceptualized and implemented a fresh, modern layout aimed at improving user engagement and visual appeal. I worked closely with fellow web designers to ensure each page aligned with the company's branding and messaging, contributing design ideas and layout improvements that elevated the overall user experience.

My Role

In the development of the Sixpent Careers and Innovations Pages, my role focused on translating concept designs from Figma into fully functional web pages using WordPress and Elementor. I collaborated closely with a team of designers, contributing to the creation of visually appealing and cohesive layouts across all sections of the website. I also implemented responsive design techniques to ensure the site delivers a consistent and user-friendly experience across various devices and screen sizes.
